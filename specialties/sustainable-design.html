Sustainable Design

Transforming building projects through innovative, eco-friendly design solutions

#DesignForSustainability

OVIA Inc. provides comprehensive Sustainable Design services that help businesses and organizations create environmentally responsible spaces. From green architecture to energy-efficient systems, we design solutions that reduce environmental impact while optimizing performance. Our approach integrates sustainable building practices with renewable energy solutions to create buildings that are not only functional but also eco-friendly.

Our Sustainable Design Expertise

  • Green Architecture: Designing spaces with a focus on sustainability and minimizing environmental impact.
  • Energy-Efficient Building Systems: Integrating efficient HVAC, lighting, and insulation systems to reduce energy consumption.
  • Renewable Energy Integration: Implementing solar, wind, and other renewable energy sources to power buildings sustainably.
  • Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA): Assessing the ecological footprint of building materials and systems.
  • LEED Certification Guidance: Helping projects achieve LEED certification to promote green building practices.
  • Water Conservation Strategies: Designing systems that reduce water usage and promote sustainable practices.

Sustainable Design Use Cases

  • Commercial Buildings: Creating energy-efficient, sustainable office spaces that enhance productivity while minimizing carbon footprints.
  • Residential Projects: Designing eco-friendly homes with sustainable materials and renewable energy solutions.
  • Retail Spaces: Incorporating sustainable design principles to create stores that appeal to eco-conscious consumers.
  • Public Buildings: Enhancing public facilities with green building strategies that reduce operational costs and environmental impact.
  • Educational Institutions: Designing campuses that promote sustainability through green infrastructure and energy-saving technologies.

Tools & Methodologies We Use

  • Energy Modeling Software: EnergyPlus, eQUEST for simulating and optimizing building energy use.
  • Building Information Modeling (BIM): Autodesk Revit for creating accurate building models and simulations.
  • Solar Design Tools: PVsyst, SAM for effective solar energy integration.
  • LEED Standards: Helping projects meet LEED certification criteria for sustainability.
  • Environmental Impact Tools: SimaPro, GaBi for assessing the environmental impact of materials and designs.
  • Water Efficiency Tools: LEED Water Efficiency credit guidelines for reducing water consumption in buildings.
